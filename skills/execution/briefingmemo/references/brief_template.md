# Brief Template

## Required Sections

### Situation/Debrief
Clear description of the current situation, context, and background. Include relevant history and current state.

### Stakes
What is at risk with this decision? Include:
- Financial implications
- Strategic importance
- Impact on stakeholders
- Opportunity costs

### Constraints
All applicable constraints:
- **Time constraints**: Decision deadlines
- **Budget constraints**: Financial limitations
- **Legal constraints**: Regulatory requirements
- **Operational constraints**: Resource limitations
- **Market constraints**: External factors

### Key Questions
Specific questions the committee must answer. Maximum 5 primary questions.

### Context Files
Attach relevant documents:
- Business metrics
- Product overview
- Market analysis
- Technical specifications
- Financial statements

## Example Brief Structure

```markdown
# Acquisition Offer - Neutral Holdings

## Situation
Neutral Holdings, a private equity firm specializing in supplement industry rollups, has made a formal offer to acquire Blend Stack for $12M cash (11x ARR). The offer is non-negotiable and expires in 30 days.

## Stakes
- **Financial**: $12M exit vs continued independent growth
- **Strategic**: Potential for faster scale under PE ownership
- **Team**: Retention and earnout structure implications
- **Market**: Competitive positioning in DTC supplement space

## Constraints
- **Time**: Decision required within 30 days
- **Budget**: Due diligence costs limited to $50K
- **Legal**: Must maintain compliance with FDA regulations
- **Operational**: Must ensure service continuity during transition

## Key Questions
1. Does the $12M valuation fairly reflect our growth potential?
2. Can we achieve better outcomes through continued independent operation?
3. What are the risks of rejecting the offer?
4. How would this impact our team and culture?
5. Are there strategic alternatives to consider?

## Context Files
- [business_metrics_q1_2025.md]
- [product_roadmap_2025.md]
- [financial_projections_2025-2027.md]
```
