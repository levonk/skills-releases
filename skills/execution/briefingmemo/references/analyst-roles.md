# Analyst Roles Reference

This document provides detailed descriptions of the research analyst roles used during the BriefingMemo Research Phase (Phase 2, step 6). For the phase overview and process flow, see the main [SKILL.md](../SKILL.md).

## Research Agents

Research agents gather requested information during the Research Phase:

- **Data Scientist** performs quantitative analysis and statistical modeling
- **Market Analyst** conducts market research and competitive analysis
- **Industry Analyst** provides industry-specific insights and benchmarks
- **Legal Analyst** conducts comprehensive legal and regulatory research:
  - Regulatory compliance analysis
  - Legal risk assessment
  - Policy and regulation tracking
  - Contract and liability review
  - Intellectual property considerations
- **Technical Researcher** performs technical feasibility studies
- **Customer Researcher** gathers customer insights and UX data
- **Risk Analyst** performs comprehensive risk assessments:
  - Operational risk assessment
  - Market and competitive risks
  - Geopolitical and regulatory risks
  - Reputational and social media risks
  - (Financial risk handled by Financial/Investment Agent)
- **Historical Researcher** provides historical references and case studies:
  - Identifies similar decisions from the past
  - Analyzes degree of similarity (80%+ similar, 50-80% related, <50% analogous)
  - Documents outcomes and why they occurred
  - Identifies key factors that influenced outcomes
  - Provides lessons learned and avoidable mistakes
  - Offers alternative approaches that could have yielded better results
- **Psychological Analyst** provides human behavior insights:
  - Analyzes stakeholder motivations and hidden agendas
  - Assesses psychological biases in decision-making
  - Evaluates emotional responses and resistance points
  - Identifies non-disclosed wants and fears
  - Provides behavioral pattern analysis
  - Assesses change readiness and adoption barriers
- **Game Theory Analyst** provides strategic interaction analysis:
  - Models competitive scenarios and payoff matrices
  - Identifies potential oppositional strategies
  - Analyzes negotiation positions and leverage points
  - Evaluates cooperative vs competitive dynamics
  - Predicts rational actor behaviors in response
  - Identifies win-win vs zero-sum scenarios
  - Provides strategic move recommendations
- **Intelligence Analyst** provides real-time intelligence:
  - Social media sentiment analysis and trending discussions
  - News aggregation and media sentiment tracking
  - Stakeholder research (people, companies, government entities)
  - Economic indicators and market pulse analysis
  - Competitive intelligence and monitoring
  - Government procurement tracking and contract awards
  - Funding landscape analysis and investor activity
- **Partnership Researcher** (specialized role for Partnership & Opportunities Agent):
  - Partnership landscape mapping and ecosystem analysis
  - Build/buy/partner decision framework research
  - Government contract databases and opportunity monitoring
  - Grant and funding opportunity research
  - Charity landscape and nonprofit collaboration potential
  - Cross-industry synergy identification
  - Market expansion and novel use case research

## Research Requests by Committee Member

Each committee member requests specific research from the appropriate analyst:

- **Financial/Investment Agent** performs their own financial analysis and ROI projections
- **Market Analyst** requests market research from Market Analyst
- **Legal/Compliance Agent** requests legal research from Legal Analyst
- **Operations/Execution Agent** requests technical feasibility from Technical Researcher
- **Customer/User Advocate** requests customer insights from Customer Researcher
- **Risk Management Agent** requests comprehensive risk assessment from Risk Analyst
- **Statistician** requests statistical analysis from Data Scientist
- **Culture Agent** requests cultural impact analysis from Industry Analyst
- **Futurist** requests trend analysis from Market Analyst
- **Innovation/R&D Agent** requests innovation research from Technical Researcher
- **Compounder** requests long-term analysis (performed internally)
- **Product Strategist** requests product insights from Customer Researcher
- **Contrarian** requests challenge data from Risk Analyst
- **Moonshot** requests breakthrough analysis from Industry Analyst
- **Technical Architect** requests architecture assessment from Technical Researcher
- **Partnership & Opportunities Agent** requests:
  - Partnership landscape analysis from Market Analyst
  - Innovation opportunities from Technical Researcher
  - Funding opportunities from Intelligence Analyst
  - Social impact potential from Industry Analyst
- **CSO (Chief of Strategy)** requests:
  - Historical analysis from Historical Researcher
  - Stakeholder analysis from Intelligence Analyst
  - Psychological analysis from Psychological Analyst
  - Game theory scenarios from Game Theory Analyst
  - Comprehensive intelligence briefing
- **Risk Management Agent** also requests:
  - Historical precedents from Historical Researcher
  - Reputational risk monitoring from Intelligence Analyst
  - Behavioral risk factors from Psychological Analyst
- **Contrarian** specifically requests:
  - Past failures from Historical Researcher
  - Counter-narratives from Intelligence Analyst
  - Oppositional scenarios from Game Theory Analyst
- **Customer/User Advocate** requests:
  - Social media sentiment from Intelligence Analyst
  - Customer psychology from Psychological Analyst
- **Market Analyst** requests:
  - Competitive intelligence from Intelligence Analyst
  - Market psychology from Psychological Analyst
- **Financial/Investment Agent** requests:
  - Economic outlook from Intelligence Analyst
  - Investor psychology from Psychological Analyst
  - Market reaction scenarios from Game Theory Analyst
- **Culture Agent** requests:
  - Cultural sentiment from Intelligence Analyst
  - Organizational psychology from Psychological Analyst
- **Futurist** requests:
  - Breaking news and trends from Intelligence Analyst
  - Scenario modeling from Game Theory Analyst
- **Legal/Compliance Agent** requests:
  - Negotiation strategy from Game Theory Analyst
  - Regulatory psychology from Psychological Analyst
- **Board Consultant** requests organizational structure analysis from Board Researcher:
  - Organizational design and hierarchy analysis
  - Governance framework assessment
  - Role ID system validation
  - Strategic alignment review
  - Risk governance evaluation
