# Sourcing Guide by Product Category

## Electronics & Computers

| Channel | When to use | Typical savings | Notes |
|---------|------------|-----------------|-------|
| Manufacturer refurbished | Always check first | 15–40% | Often includes full warranty |
| [Back Market](https://www.backmarket.com/) | Phones, laptops, tablets | 20–50% | Graded condition system |
| [Swappa](https://swappa.com/) | Phones, laptops, watches | 15–35% | Verified devices, no junk |
| Amazon Warehouse | Open-box / returned | 10–30% | "Used - Like New" is often pristine |
| Best Buy Open Box | In-store only | 10–25% | Negotiable at store level |
| B-stock / liquidation | Bulk or high risk tolerance | 30–60% | [B-Stock](https://bstock.com/), [Liquidation.com](https://www.liquidation.com/) |
| eBay (certified refurbished) | When warranty matters | 15–30% | Allstate 2-year warranty included |

**Never buy used**: Batteries below 80% health, items with water damage indicators tripped, counterfeit-prone items (cables, chargers, memory cards).

## Vehicles

| Channel | When to use | Typical savings | Notes |
|---------|------------|-----------------|-------|
| Private party (Craigslist, FB Marketplace) | Best price, know what you're buying | 10–25% vs dealer | No dealer markup; get PPI |
| [GovPlanet](https://www.govplanet.com/) | Trucks, fleet vehicles | 20–50% | Government maintained |
| [GSA Auctions](https://gsaauctions.gov/) | Federal surplus vehicles | 30–60% | High mileage but maintained |
| [Copart](https://www.copart.com/) / [IAAI](https://www.iaai.com/) | Salvage / project cars | 50–80% | Insurance auction; title issues |
| Manufacturer CPO | When warranty matters | 5–15% vs new | Extended factory warranty |
| Rental car sales ([Enterprise](https://www.enterprisecarsales.com/)) | Reliable recent models | 15–25% | Higher miles, well-maintained |

**Mileage thresholds to avoid**:
- CVT transmissions: avoid 100k+ miles
- Turbo engines: avoid 80k+ without maintenance records
- Diesel particulate filters: avoid 150k+ without regen history
- Timing belt engines: buy only if belt was replaced at interval or budget $800–$1,500 for it

## Home & Appliances

| Channel | When to use | Typical savings | Notes |
|---------|------------|-----------------|-------|
| [Habitat ReStore](https://www.habitat.org/restores) | Appliances, building materials | 30–70% | Donated items; condition varies |
| Scratch & dent outlets | Cosmetic damage OK | 20–50% | Full manufacturer warranty |
| Costco | Large appliances | 5–15% + included delivery/install | Extended warranty included |
| Floor model clearance | End of model year | 15–40% | Negotiate at big-box stores |
| Estate sales | Quality furniture, vintage | 30–80% | [EstateSales.net](https://www.estatesales.net/) |
| Buy Nothing groups | Free | 100% | Facebook groups, [BuyNothing app](https://buynothingproject.org/) |

## Luxury Goods

| Channel | When to use | Typical savings | Notes |
|---------|------------|-----------------|-------|
| Authorized grey market | Watches, bags | 10–30% | [Jomashop](https://www.jomashop.com/), [Chrono24](https://www.chrono24.com/) |
| Consignment | Designer clothing, accessories | 30–60% | [The RealReal](https://www.therealreal.com/), [Vestiaire](https://www.vestiairecollective.com/) |
| Auction houses | High-value collectibles | Variable | [Christie's](https://www.christies.com/), [Heritage](https://www.ha.com/) |
| Outlet stores | Current-season overflow | 20–40% | Beware outlet-only (lower quality) lines |
