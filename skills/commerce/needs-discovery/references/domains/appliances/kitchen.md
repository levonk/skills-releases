# Kitchen Appliance Buying Constraints

> Dishwasher, range, oven, pizza oven. What's unique vs generic appliances:
**cookware compatibility** (induction), **noise** (dishwasher dB), and
**venting** (gas range).

## Dishwasher

### Noise (dB)

| dB rating | Perception |
|-----------|-----------|
| < 44 | Very quiet — near silent |
| 44-50 | Quiet — normal conversation audible |
| 50+ | Noisy — noticeable during operation |

- Under 44 dB is worth the premium for open-plan kitchens.

### Capacity & Features

| Feature | Guidance |
|---------|----------|
| Place settings | 12+ standard; 14-16 for large families |
| Third rack | Utensils/small items — frees lower racks |
| Hard food disposer | No pre-rinse, noisier, more water |
| Filter | Quieter, needs periodic cleaning (rinse monthly) |
| Stainless tub | Dries better, quieter, more durable than plastic |

### Installation

- Needs dedicated **water line**, **drain connection**, and **electrical**
  (hardwire or cord-and-plug, depending on local code).
- Confirm height — tall tubs need a full 34.5" cutout.

## Range / Oven — Fuel

| Fuel | Pros | Cons |
|------|------|------|
| Gas | Responsive heat, works in outage (burners) | Gas line, venting required, combustion byproducts |
| Electric (coil/radiant) | Simple, cheaper | Slower response, no outage operation |
| Induction | Fast, efficient, cool surface, safe | Needs **magnetic cookware** (cast iron, magnetic stainless); $200-1400 more |
| Dual fuel | Gas cooktop + electric oven | Best of both; $1800+; needs gas + 240V |

- **Induction cookware test**: a magnet must stick to the bottom.

### Oven Features

| Feature | Notes |
|---------|-------|
| Convection | Fan circulates air — even baking, faster, lower temp (~25°F) |
| Self-clean (pyrolytic) | Burns off residue at ~900°F — smells, smoke, can damage control boards |
| Steam clean | Lower-temp alternative, lighter soils only |
| Probe thermometer | Cook to internal temp — set-and-forget for roasts |
| True vs fan convection | True convection has a third heating element behind the fan |

- Self-clean: run it **before** a big cook, ventilate the kitchen, and don't
  use it on newer ovens with sensitive electronics if the manual warns against
  it.

## Pizza Oven

| Factor | Guidance |
|--------|----------|
| Indoor vs outdoor | Indoor: electric, venting may be needed; outdoor: wood/gas, weatherproof |
| Max temp | **700°F minimum** for Neapolitan; **900°F+** ideal (60-90 sec pies) |
| Fuel | Wood (traditional flavor, tending), gas (convenient, consistent), electric (precise), pellet (hybrid) |
| Stone vs steel | Stone: traditional, absorbs moisture; steel: faster, better conduction, home-oven hack |
| Preheat time | 15-30 min to reach 800°F+ — plan ahead |
| Footprint | Countertop units ~16-20" deep; freestanding outdoor 30-40" |

- Outdoor wood-fired ovens may require **clearance to combustibles** and local
  burn restrictions — check before buying.

## Output Format Example

```
Dishwasher: 44 dB, 14 place settings, third rack, stainless tub, filter type
Range: 30" induction, requires magnetic cookware, 240V/40A
Oven: true convection, probe thermometer, steam-clean (avoid pyrolytic on this model)
Pizza oven: outdoor gas, 900°F max, cordierite stone, 20 min preheat
```
